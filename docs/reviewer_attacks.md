# Reviewer Attacks

- This is only object tracking under occlusion with robot geometry added.
- The strongest mean result is not decisive because paired CI crosses zero against the closest non-oracle baseline.
- Ablations are too close to show that the self-mask and branch belief are necessary.
- False disappearance remains high on combined stress.
- The evidence is MuJoCo-only and may not transfer to real cameras or robot arms.
- No external benchmark or real hardware validates the visibility model.
- Hostile prior work already crowds scene memory, manipulation world models, and failure-aware robotic manipulation.

Response: these attacks are valid. The correct action is to archive as a real negative result, not to claim ICLR-main readiness.
